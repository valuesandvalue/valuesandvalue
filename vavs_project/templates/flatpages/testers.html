If you are joining the project as a tester there are a few additional steps involved. Each step is quite simple and the whole process should not take more than a few minutes. Thank you for your help in contributing to the project.

  1. You will receive an invite to join the project on your Facebook account, please check for a notification, sent either from Beverley Skeggs or Simon Yuill, similar to this:

    You may have to look back into older messages.

  2. Click on this to view the invite, click Confirm to accept:

    You must confirm the invite before you can register with the project.

  3. Go to the Values & Value login page and click the blue Log in with Facebook button.

  4. Login with your Facebook email and password, this will register your account with Values & Value. As we are in test mode you see a warning similar to this:

    This is normal during the test phase for a Facebook app, if you are concerned about the permissions being used please check our Data Collection and Privacy Policy.

  5. Once you are registered, follow the instructions to install our Admonitor Firefox Add-on.

If you have any problems or questions please contact us.
