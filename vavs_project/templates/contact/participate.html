For our research on modern digital relations we are looking for input and participation from people using Facebook.

There are two ways of being involved, as part of the detailed study, or by sending us your comments on Facebook.

For the detailed study, we would like to follow your online Facebook activity and the advertising that appears as you browse the web, and interview you about your experiences with Facebook. This will take place over a period of three months and to take part we will ask you to install two pieces of software we have created for the project. You can read about these on the software page. All information that we gather will be kept anonymous and you will have full private access to this.
